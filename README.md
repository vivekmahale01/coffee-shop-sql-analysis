This project analyzes coffee shop sales data using SQL to generate business insights and identify the best cities for future expansion.

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains four tables:

- City
- Customers
- Products
- Sales

These tables are connected using primary and foreign key relationships.

## Business Questions Solved

1. Total Revenue Generated
2. Total Customers
3. Revenue by City
4. Customers by City
5. Top Selling Products
6. Revenue by Product
7. Average Sale Amount
8. Highest Spending Customers
9. Average Customer Spending by City
10. Best City for Expansion

## SQL Concepts Used

- SELECT
- WHERE
- GROUP BY
- ORDER BY
- COUNT()
- SUM()
- AVG()
- INNER JOIN
- Aggregate Functions

## Key Insights

- Identified top revenue-generating cities
- Found the most popular products
- Analyzed customer spending patterns
- Compared city-wise business performance
- Evaluated expansion opportunities using data

## Final Recommendation

Based on revenue, customer base, and spending behavior analysis:

### 1. Pune
- Highest revenue generation
- Strong customer spending
- Good market potential

### 2. Chennai
- Strong revenue performance
- Consistent customer demand
- Balanced growth opportunity

### 3. Jaipur
- High customer count
- Strong growth potential
- Efficient business performance
